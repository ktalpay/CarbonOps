## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in CarbonOps a harassment-free, inclusive, and respectful experience for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

### Positive behaviors:
- Showing empathy and kindness  
- Being respectful of differing opinions  
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback  
- Focusing on what benefits the community  
- Gracefully accepting responsibility for mistakes  

### Unacceptable behaviors:
- Harassment or discrimination  
- Public or private bullying  
- Trolling, insults, or derogatory comments  
- Publishing private information  
- Threats of violence  

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.  
They may take appropriate corrective action, including:

- Warning the offender  
- Temporary or permanent ban  
- Removal of comments, PRs, or issues
